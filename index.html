About Scott

Scott Borowiak, USPTA Certified Professional has led tennis and travel programs for over thirty years in California, Hawaii, Mexico and Europe. His knowledge and connections in local areas provide unique experiences for travelers. Scott has led numerous travel seminars, winning the United States Professional Tennis Association "Best Seminar" Award in 1995. He has been Director of Tennis at the Oakland Hills Tennis Club, Placerville Racquet Club and currently serves as the Tennis Director at Moraga Valley Swim and Tennis Club in Orinda, California.
